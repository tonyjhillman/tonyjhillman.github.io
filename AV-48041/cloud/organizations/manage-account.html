Manage Your Couchbase Capella Account

    +
    Your Couchbase Capella account has its own settings and preferences, independent of any organization.

    Your Couchbase Capella account is independent of the organizations that you are a member of. Things like your login information, contact information, and preferences are handled under your personal Capella account settings.

    Accessing Your Account Settings and Preferences

    You can reach your account settings and preferences by clicking on your name in the upper-right corner of the Capella UI, and then clicking Account or Preferences, respectively.

    Manage Account Settings

    To manage your Couchbase Capella account settings, log into the Capella UI and click on your name in the upper-right corner. In the drop-down menu that appears, click Account. This opens the Settings > Account tab:

    Update Your Contact Information

    1. In the Contact Information section, click Edit.

      This opens the the Contact Information fly-out menu.

    2. Edit your contact information details.

      Name

      This is the full name that appears to other users in all of your organizations.

      Primary Email

      The email address that you use to log into Capella, and which notifications are sent to.

      Phone Number

      The phone number that you can be reached at should you engage with Couchbase Support.

    3. Click Update Contact Information to save your changes.

      If you change your primary email address, a confirmation email will be sent to the new email address. You will need to confirm the change by clicking the confirmation link in the email.

    Change Your Password

    1. In the Security section, click Update Password.

      This opens the Security fly-out menu.

    2. Enter and confirm a new password, and then click Update Password to save your changes.

    Password can also be reset from the log-in screen, by selecting Forgot Password to get a password recovery email. Only one password reset email will be sent in each one minute period, regardless of how many times the Forgot Password is clicked.

    Multi-Factor Authentication

    We strongly recommend setting up Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) for all users on your Capella account. This can be done from the Security section (see above) by clicking Enable MFA. This produces the following flyout:

    You will need an MFA app installed on your cellphone — we have tested with Google Authenticator, Authy, 1Password, and LastPass Authenticator. Other apps such as Microsoft Authenticator, which follow the de facto standard of a Tx interval of 30 seconds, should also work just as well. Scan the 2D barcode with the MFA app, and a 6-digit code will be displayed. Enter this here and click Enable.

    You will now need to use the MFA app to generate a time-limited one-time password (TOTP) each time that you authenticate, giving you considerably more protection against a number of common malicious attack types.

    Account Preferences

    To manage your Capella preferences, log into the Capella UI and click on your name in the upper-right corner. In the drop-down menu that appears, click Preferences. This opens the Settings > Preferences tab:

    Update Your Region and Time

    1. In the Region & Time section, click Edit.

      This opens the the Region & Time fly-out menu.

    2. Configure your region and time details.

      Region

      The region in which you reside.

      Time Zone

      The time zone in which you reside.

    3. Click Update Region & Time to save your changes.

    Update Your Email Notifications

    1. In the Email Notifications section, click Edit.

      This opens the Email Notifications fly-out menu.

    2. Check the boxes for the desired notification settings.

    3. Click Update Email Notifications to save your changes.

    Close Your Account

    In order to close your Couchbase Capella account, you will first need to leave all organizations of which your account is a member.

    Your personal Couchbase Capella account can be a member of multiple organizations. If you want to leave one organization, but remain a member of another, you can simply remove yourself from an individual organization. You are allowed to remove yourself from any and all organizations, so long as there remains at least one user in each organization that has the Organization Owner role.

    If you are in charge of an organization and its billing obligations, you will need to either hand off those obligations to other users, or you will need to delete the organization entirely. Handing off your obligations to other users simply means ensuring that the proper users have been promoted to the Owner role, and that the organization’s billing information is up to date.

    If you leave all organizations, your account will still remain active, but you will no longer be able to log into the Capella UI. Only once you’ve been re-invited to an organization will you be able to log in again.

    Contact Couchbase Capella Support to Finalize Account Deletion

    Once you leave all organizations, your account will still remain active. To completely delete your account, you will need to contact the Couchbase Capella Support team to perform the final deletion.

    Once the Support team deletes your account, you will no longer be able to use the same email address to create a new Capella account. Likewise, Capella users will no longer be able to use that same email address to invite you to their organizations.
